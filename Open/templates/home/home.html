What Open is

OPEN will be the modern way to setup blind dates.

Common dating apps often fail their users in one simple manner- they rely on quick, snap judgements, usually based on appearances.

Open will result in deeper, less superficial meet ups- for people "open" to new experiences.

How It Works

OPEN aims to fill the niche of a "smart" dating app.

OPEN will be similar to other dating apps by matching users primarily by shared interests.

What Is Different About Open

However, it also will allow users to facilitate the app during the date (by suggesting topics of conversation, even customized date ideas) and rate their dates afterwards.

This breadth of information will enable OPEN to have more accurate predictions about who will make a great match.
